About Us

Roam-Bot is an AI-powered itinerary planner designed to simplify travel planning through intelligent automation. We help travelers create personalized journeys based on destination, travel dates, and real-time weather insights.

Our platform is built for solo travelers, families, and groups who want efficient, flexible, and smart travel plans without the stress of manual research. From destination suggestions to daily activity planning, Roam-Bot does the thinking for you.

By combining artificial intelligence, data analysis, and a user-friendly interface, Roam-Bot ensures accurate recommendations, optimized schedules, and a smooth planning experience — even in offline scenarios.

Travel smarter, explore deeper, and focus on experiences — Roam-Bot takes care of the plan.
